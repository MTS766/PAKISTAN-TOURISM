WELCOME TO GOLDEN SAND BEACH

Golden Sand Beach is a stunningly beautiful beach located in the city of Gwadar in the southwestern province of Balochistan in Pakistan. The beach is known for its golden-colored sand which stretches for miles along the Arabian Sea. It is a popular destination for both locals and tourists who come to enjoy the warm waters and picturesque views. The beach is a great spot for swimming, sunbathing, and leisurely walks along its long stretch of sand. Visitors can also enjoy a range of water-based activities, such as jetskiing and boating, or simply relax in one of the beach cafes that line the shore.
