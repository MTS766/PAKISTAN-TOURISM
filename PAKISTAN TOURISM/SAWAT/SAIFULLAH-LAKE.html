WELCOME TO SAIFULLAH LAKE

Saifullah Lake (Urdu: سیف اللہ جھیل) also known as Saifullah Danda (Pashto: سیف اللہ ڈھنڈہ) in Pashto is an alpine glacial lake located to the north of Kalam Valley in Swat District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. From Mahodand Lake, you can trek to Saifullah Lake or also access the lake through 4x4 vehicles. The trek to Saifullah Lake is just about 10-15 minutes.
